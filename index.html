About

    I'm a Ph.D. student at Purdue ECE, working with Felix Xiaozhu Lin. I'm interested in Operating systems, distributed systems, runtime systems, and computer architecture. Recently, I have built systems for data analytics to harness modern hardware, including multicore (56-core) and hybrid memory (3D-Stacked memory & DRAM). I had played with OS kernel (Linux memory subsystem & drivers), system virtualization (QEMU & KVM), and RPC systems (with RDMA) when I was a graduate student at Institute of Computing Technology (ICT), Chinese Academy of Sciences (CAS).
